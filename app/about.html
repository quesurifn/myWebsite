About Me

Hello! My name is Kyle Fahey and I am currently a salesmen for AT&T in Chicago, Illinois. My next goal in life is to become a Front End Developer. I've always had a passion for coding. I remember when I started at 13 years old, I often played hooky from class to teach myself how make a C++ program that could help me do my math homework. While I never got it to do Algebra, I did accomplish a working program that helped me with my homework and definately got showed off during the lunch hour. My next program I further dove into C++, building an elapsed time calculator. It took user input for two dates, coverted those to julian dates using ctime and then spat out the amount of time that had elapsed. This is how my love affair with programming began.


Since then I've accomplished some great things inside the tech world. HTC, the smartphone manufactuer recruited me to their brand ambassading program, HTC Elevate, then later into their beta tester program. I wound up as a recruit among volunteers testing for software bugs in the new Android software updates before the updates webkit-border-radiuspushed to AT&T. Later I found myself as an editor for Droid Now, a blog devoted to all things android. In 2014 we were invited to and attended Mobile World Congress in Barcelona, Spain.


While it may be hard to believe, I at times venture outside of tech to shoot photos by Lake Michigan, cook amazing meals or plan the next big trade for my portfolio. Over the past few years I have found much joy in learning and mastering new skills such as Gardening, Cooking, Photography and Fishing.
